About Cabatuan

Cabatuan is a municipality in Iloilo, Philippines, known for its historical significance and cultural heritage. The Tinuom Festival is a major event, celebrating the town's unique culinary tradition and rich history.

Festivals

Tinuom Festival

The Tinuom Festival is a celebration of Cabatuan's culinary heritage, featuring the famous Tinuom dish and honoring the town's patron saint, San Nicolas de Tolentino.
