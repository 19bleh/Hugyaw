About Barotac Viejo

Barotac Viejo is a municipality in Iloilo, Philippines, known for its rich agricultural heritage and vibrant festivals. The Patubas Festival is a highlight, celebrating the town's bountiful harvests and cultural traditions.

Festivals

Patubas Festival

The Patubas Festival is a celebration of Barotac Viejo's agricultural abundance, showcasing the town's gratitude for a bountiful harvest and its rich cultural heritage.
