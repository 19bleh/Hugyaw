About Barotac Viejo

Located in Iloilo, Barotac Viejo is a picturesque municipality renowned for its rich agricultural heritage. The town thrives on its agricultural success, earning its title as the Harvest Capital of Iloilo. Home to lush farmlands and a close-knit community, Barotac Viejo celebrates its agricultural roots with pride and joy through its vibrant festivals and cultural activities.

Festivals

Patubas Festival

Patubas Festival is Barotac Viejo's grand celebration of its agricultural abundance and cultural identity. The term "Patubas" translates to "harvest" in Hiligaynon, reflecting the town's gratitude for a bountiful yield. This lively event features colorful street dances, agricultural fairs, and cultural presentations that highlight the hard work and unity of the local farming community.
